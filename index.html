Who We Are

About Bad Debt Solutions

Bad Debt Solutions (BDS) is a professional debt collection and recovery agency based in Accra, Ghana. We help businesses convert unpaid receivables into recovered cash while preserving valuable client relationships.

Our dual-phase approach begins with amicable, pre-legal negotiations and escalates to judicial recovery only when necessary. This ensures maximum recovery with minimum disruption to your business relationships.

Fully compliant with Data Protection Act (Act 843)
Deep knowledge of Ghanaian laws and business culture
Transparent, ethical debt recovery practices

Relationship-First Approach

We understand that your clients are valuable. Our methods prioritize preserving business relationships while ensuring debt recovery.
